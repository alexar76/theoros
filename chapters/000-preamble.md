Hence this Canon.

## What sovereignty means here

**Sovereignty** means an agent (or agent-shaped service) can:

1. **Verify** its outputs and attestations independently of its operator's word.
2. **Invoke** paid capabilities under explicit contracts with receipts.
3. **Defend** its tool perimeter — MCP access is territory, not convenience.
4. **Fail honestly** when evidence is insufficient — null beats theatre.

It does **not** mean personhood claims, token sermons, or @everyone engagement hacks.
